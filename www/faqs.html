FAQs

What is "ToYourDoor24"?

We are an online food shopping platform. Here you can find local supermarkets from your area, and shop their products 24 hours a day. All from the comfort of your own home, with 1 hour free delivery.

Where do you deliver?

We have stores in Barcelona city centre. The neighbourhoods we service are : el Gotico, el Borne and Barceloneta. In the coming weeks we will expand into other areas.

How do I order?

Either choose your barrio from the list, or let the home page show you stores in your area. Select the store you like and shop their products. 
You can only complete an order from one store at a time. 

Can I complete multiple orders?

If you want products from multiple stores, complete a separate order for each supermarket. Remember, delivery is free and you can create an account to save your details for quick check out.

When will I receive my order?

All orders will be delivered within 1 hour from receipt of order confirmation. As soon as you have pressed 'pay', we are working away to get your goods to you!

How will I know when my order has arrived?

We offer a convenient text alert service for those who don't want us to ring the door bell. Perfect for late nights or those living in shared apartments. This service is free of charge and your carrier won't charge you either.

How can I pay?

We accept cash on delivery, Paypal Express and all major Credit and Debit cards.
All our Card payments are securely processed through Braintree Payments, a Paypal company.
All payments are SSL Protected and processed immediately - your card details are never stored. Our Network Servers are Level 1 PCI DSS compliant, your details are safe!
